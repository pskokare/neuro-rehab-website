You want to provide the best care, but balancing it with work and other responsibilities can be overwhelming.

At Apricot Rehab in Pune , our Professional Caregiverservice is designed to provide that essential support. When is a Professional Caregiver Needed?

Our caregiver services are tailored to the specific needs of your loved one. Here are some common scenarios where our support is invaluable:

Care for Neurological Conditions (Stroke, Parkinson's, etc.)

Statistic: Over 70% of stroke survivors require some assistance with daily activities after returning home. Included:

  • Assistance with walking & prescribed home exercises.
  • Help with bathing, dressing, and toileting.
  • Timely medication reminders.
  • Compassionate companionship and supervision.
  • Not Included:
  • Cooking and general housekeeping.
  • Administering injections or complex medical procedures.

Post-Surgical & Orthopedic Support at Home Included:

  • Assistance with mobility (e.g., walking with a walker).
  • Ensuring surgical precautions (e.g., for hip replacements) are followed.
  • Help with bathing and toileting when movement is restricted.
  • Monitoring for any signs of complications.
  • Not Included:
  • Cooking and general housekeeping.
  • Wound dressing changes (this requires a skilled nurse).

Elderly & Dementia Care Included:

  • Support with personal hygiene and grooming.
  • Companionship to combat loneliness and provide mental stimulation.
  • Fall prevention and mobility support.
  • Providing a safe and supervised environment for individuals with dementia.
  • Not Included:
  • Cooking and general housekeeping.
  • Managing complex medical equipment.

Respite Care for Family Caregivers Included:

  • Providing you with a much-needed break for a few hours or a full day.
  • Taking over all caregiving duties so you can rest and recharge.
  • Ensuring your loved one is safe and cared for in your absence.
  • Offering you peace of mind to focus on your own well-being.
  • Not Included:
  • Cooking and general housekeeping.
  • Transporting the patient outside the home.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Do your caregivers undergo background verification?

Yes, absolutely. Every caregiver on our team goes through a multi-point verification process, including police, court, and Aadhar checks, to ensure the utmost safety and trustworthiness.

What is the difference between a "Caregiver/Attendant" and a "Nurse"?

A Caregiver provides non-medical support, assisting with activities of daily living like bathing, walking, companionship, and medication reminders. A Skilled Nurse is a licensed medical professional who can perform centeral tasks like giving injections, managing wounds, administering IVs, and operating medical equipment.

Are your caregivers available for 24-hour care?

Yes, we can arrange for caregiver services in shifts to provide 24/7, round-the-clock support for your loved one.

What happens if our regular caregiver is sick or needs to take leave?

We understand that continuity of care is crucial. We maintain a pool of trained caregivers and will arrange for a suitable and prompt replacement to ensure there is no disruption in service.
